Common Questions

Do I need to be technical?
No. You tell us the problem. We figure out how to solve it. You'll understand what we built and how to use it.
How much does it cost?
First 15 minutes are free. Consultations are $150/hr. Custom agent builds start at $500, skills from $300, data scraping from $200. Every project is scoped and quoted upfront — no surprises.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a tool. We build systems. There's a difference between having a hammer and having a construction crew. We deploy agents connected to your data, integrated with your workflow, running without you.
What if I only need something small?
Some of our best work is a focused automation that saves someone 3 hours a week. Small doesn't mean simple. Book a call. Tell us what you're thinking.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. Monthly retainer packages starting at $500/month for maintenance, updates, and continued development. Your bot will evolve. We'll be here for it.
How fast can you build something?
Depends on complexity. A focused bot can be live in days. A multi-agent system takes weeks. Either way, you'll know the timeline before we start.
