Questions

Common Questions

Do I need to be technical to work with you?
Nope. We translate the technical stuff so you don't have to. You tell us what problem you're solving — we figure out how to build it. You'll understand what we built and how to use it.
How much does it cost?
Discovery calls are free. Consultations run $75–$150/hr. Custom builds are scoped and quoted — some projects are a few hundred dollars, some are retainer-level engagements. We'll tell you straight what it'll cost before you commit to anything.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a tool. We build systems. There's a big difference between having a hammer and having a construction crew. We deploy agents connected to your data, integrated with your workflow, and running without you babysitting them.
What if I only need something small?
Perfect. Some of our best work is a focused automation that saves someone 3 hours a week. Small doesn't mean simple — and we take it just as seriously. Book a call, tell us what you're thinking.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. We offer monthly retainer packages starting at $500/month for maintenance, updates, and continued development. If your bot needs to evolve (and it will), we're here for it.
How fast can you build something?
Depends on complexity. A focused single-purpose bot can be live in days. A full multi-agent business system takes weeks of careful architecture. Either way, you'll know the timeline before work starts.
