# Content SEO Audit - WeBot Agency + Studio

Date: 2026-06-28

## Current Content Shape

The site now has focused search-intent pages rather than relying only on one long homepage section:

- Plan & Itinerary: vacations, date nights, business get-togethers, road trips.
- Gather & Extract: scraping, OCR, PDFs, documents, spreadsheets, structured exports.
- Create & Polish: cards, social posts, email copy, brand starts, business cards.
- Build & Automate: code review, code writing, spreadsheet formulas, workflow automation.
- Review & Decide: comparison briefs, vendor summaries, fact-check packs, decision memos.

Studio now has support pages for agent jobs and pricing so Google and users can understand the checkout/delivery model without relying only on in-page anchors.

## Editorial Rules

- Keep the voice direct and practical.
- Avoid keyword stuffing.
- Do not claim CRM wiring, ad activation, affiliate links, tracking pixels, or broader storage promises unless implemented and verified.
- Keep Studio buyer-facing copy on monthly access and one-time reviewed jobs.
- Every public offer page should answer: what it does, what to provide, what comes back, how review works, and where to start.
